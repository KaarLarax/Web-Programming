Regular Show Characters

Mordecai

Mordecai is a 23-year-old blue jay. He is responsible, creative, and sometimes sarcastic. He works as a groundskeeper at the park and is best friends with Rigby. Together, they often get into trouble while trying to avoid work.

Rigby

Rigby is a 23-year-old raccoon. He is impulsive, mischievous, and loves to have fun. Rigby is Mordecai's best friend and partner in slacking off. His wild ideas often lead to crazy adventures in the park.

Benson

Benson is the park manager and Mordecai and Rigby's boss. He is a gumball machine who tries to keep order in the park. Although he gets angry at their antics, he cares about his employees and wants the best for them.
